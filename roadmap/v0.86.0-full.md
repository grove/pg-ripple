`docs/src/reference/conformance-trends.md` to plot it.

### T13-05 | MEDIUM | Effort: S

does not match the A12 gap list.
**Fix:** Re-run audit; close actual gap.

### T13-06 | LOW | Effort: S

and `merge_throughput_history.csv` existing.
**Fix:** Wire `scripts/bench_check_regression.py --fail-on-regression 10`

### T13-07 | LOW | Effort: S
