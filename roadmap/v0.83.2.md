# v0.83.2 — Assessment 13 Correctness, Performance & Code Quality

> Source: PLAN_OVERALL_ASSESSMENT_13.md  
> Status: Planned  
> Scope: Large

## Summary

v0.83.2 closes the 22 "should-fix" and medium-priority correctness, performance,
and code-quality findings from Assessment 13.  The headline items are:
`batch_decode` respecting the `strict_dictionary` GUC, the two largest remaining
module splits (`schema.rs` 1 939 lines, `federation.rs` 1 693 lines), a CI
module-size lint gate, and a VP-promotion crash-recovery regression test.
